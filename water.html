Knowledge!

During a Home Inspection when educating yourself about your investment is a perfect time to have your water tested especially if the property is on a well. Well water requires frequent testing, while public does not, we recommend testing if plans for babies are in the near future or there are any concerns such as odors, odd taste, odd color, build up on fixtures, soap not lathering, dry skin, old plumbing or if there are known nearby containments. Contact us if you have any questions or are interested in having us sample your water.

Free Water Scam?

Watch out for these companies offering a "Free Water Test" these instant in-home test always seem to find something wrong with your water but they happen to have a limited time deal on a system that costs thousands of dollars. We don't sell equipment, just provide high quality lab reports and information about your water so you can decide how to handle it, if at all.
