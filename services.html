Residential Home Inspections

We know what to look for and will generate a detailed report on the home's systems and components highlighting items of concern, as well as an informational home maintenance book for reference so you can spend more time with your family and less time worrying.

What we check for

A non-invasive, confidential, visual examination of the accessible areas of a residential property, this is designed to identify defects within specific systems and components.
