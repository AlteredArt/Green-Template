Spring Maintenance Checklist

  • Clean gutter & downspouts
  • Service Air Conditioner
  • Trim Vegetation away from house
  • Check roof, flashings and sealant for damages
  • Prep sprinkler system
  • Pressure wash exterior / clean windows
  • Clean dryer vent / check all vents
  • Inspect smoke detectors
  • Inspect Fire Extinguishers
  • Check for water leaks

Winter in the Rockies

Here in Colorado snow and ice build up can damage your roof over the winter. Make sure to have someone climb up there to check for new leaks, missing sealant flashing and shingles.
